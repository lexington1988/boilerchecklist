Boiler Service Checklist

  • Check Manufacturer's Instructions for service requirements and relevant boiler readings
  • Visually Inspect Boiler, Flue and Gas System
  • Check Fuse
  • Perform Electrical Safe To Touch Test and Safe Isolation To TB118a (Prove, Test, Prove)
  • Perform Relevant Boiler Service Procedure As Per Manufacturer's Instructions
  • Visually Inspect Inside Of Boiler (Cable Grommets, Room Seal, Burner Insulation etc.)
  • Perform Combustion Analysis Comparing To Manufacturer's Instructions (Case On)
  • Perform Gas Rate And Compare To Manufacturer's Instructions
  • Test FFD
  • Perform Full Tightness Test Procedure (While Boiler In Lockout State)
  • Spray Test Nipple And ECV Barrel
  • Test Alarms And Check Expiry Dates
  • Ensure Boiler Has Been Reassembled Correctly, Flue Sample Point Has Been Replaced And That Appliance Is Left In Working Order
  • Inspect Gas Meter Test Nipple Final Time To Ensure No Leaks Are Left
  • Collect Tenants Contact Details
